What is ScamCheck?

ScamCheck is an anti-scam toolkit for crypto users. It normalizes signals from explorers and public APIs and generates instant red-flag detection. Use it before joining a token, funding a wallet, or following a group invite.

  • Chains: Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Avalanche, TRON, Solana, XRP and more.
  • Fast checks via our API; full reports and priority in Premium.
  • Responsive UI for mobile and desktop.
